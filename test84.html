Snaptube Installation Guide

Thanks for downloading Snaptube!


Once you have downloaded the APK file, launch it, and complete the installation of Snaptube app by following the steps below:


Step 1: Locate the Downloaded APK
  • 1. Click on the popup that appears in your browser when the download finishes
  • 2. Find it in the "Downloads" of your browser
  • 3. Find it in the notification bar of your phone
Step 2: Confirm Safety If a popup appears in your browser, click "OK". Snaptube is safe for your mobile.


Snaptube is safe for your mobile

Security Verified
Step 3: Enable Unknown Sources During the Snaptube app installation process, you may need to go to your device's "Settings" and turn on "Unknown Sources".


This is required because Snaptube is not on Google Play. Turning on this option won't harm your mobile.
